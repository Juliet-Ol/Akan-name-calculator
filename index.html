Akan name calculator

Akan is a web application that uses your birthday to calculate the day of the week you were born. This application is inspired by Ghana a country in West Africa where they use days of the week to name a newborn. Children are given names depending on which day of the week they were born. They are the different set names for male and female.

In Ghana the first name frequently corresponds to the day one was born and gender.

Akan Name

For example a male child born on sunday will be called Kwasi the female child will be called Akosua
